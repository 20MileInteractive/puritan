Dress, dine and de-compress at our flagship location.

Our 20,000 square foot flagship store is located on Main Street, Hyannis. Cape Cod's premier specialty store with an impressive selection of designer brand-named apparel and footwear for men and women. Experience Puritan Outdoors featuring skis, snowboards, tennis, and technical apparel.

Solstice Day Spa

After shopping, stop downstairs at Solstice Day Spa to be pampered with manicures, hair cuts, facials, and massage therapy. Amenities include four massage rooms designed to accommodate couples massages, a large steam shower, custom-built pedicure chairs, a private hair salon area with five stylists, and even a "sanctuary room" where customers can enjoy healthy refreshments.
