The first Vineyard Vines concept store opened on Cape Cod in April 2005. Very quickly, our customers told us we needed more space to show the full collection of vineyard vines clothing and accessories for men, women, and children.

In May of 2009, we tripled our space to 1,500 sq. ft. For a big taste of the good life, come visit us at 21 Market Street in Mashpee Commons, on Cape Cod.
